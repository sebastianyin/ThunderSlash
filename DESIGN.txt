Game Plan

Genre:  Top Down Scrolling Shooter

Name:  Thunder Strike

Goal:  Winning condition - player survive through level 1 and eradicate the final boss in level 2
       Losing condition - player's lifeLeft drop to 0

Mechanics:  Player use "UP", "DOWN", "LEFT", "RIGHT" key to move the player's aircraft to evade the missles from enemy,
            use "D" key to fire missle.

Level:  1st level - mob level (all enemys are mobs which never shoot and can be killed with one missle)
        2nd level - boss level (one big boss which can fire missles and sustain the damage of multiple missles)
